  # Privacy Policy

  **Effective date:** 11 May 2026
  **Last updated:** 11 May 2026

  This policy describes how Looper ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses,
   and protects information when you use the Looper mobile application.

  Looper is an independent app developed by Darrel Brown in Powell River,
  British Columbia, Canada.

  ## Information we collect

  ### Information you provide
  - **Profile** — your name, handicap, handedness, preferred shot shape,
  common miss types, and avatar choice.
  - **Bag setup** — the clubs in your bag and their typical distances.
  - **Round data** — scores, shots logged, GPS positions during rounds,
  courses played, and notes.
  - **Feedback** — any messages you submit via the in-app feedback form,
  with your device model and OS version.
  - **Coaching messages** — questions you ask the AI caddie during rounds.
  - **Account credentials (optional)** — if you sign in with
  email/password or Google, we receive your email address and (for Google)
   your name and profile photo.

  ### Information collected automatically
  - **Device location** — GPS coordinates during active rounds, used to
  calculate distances and recommend clubs. We do not track your location
  when you're not in a round.
  - **Device information** — operating system version, device model, and
  app version, used for diagnostics.
  - **Crash and performance data** — collected if the app crashes, via
  Firebase Crashlytics.
  - **Usage analytics** — basic events like "round started" or "feedback
  submitted", via Firebase Analytics, used to understand how the app is
  being used.
  - **Anonymous account identifier** — when you first install the app, we
  create an anonymous Firebase account so your data can be stored. This
  identifier doesn't contain any personal information.

  ## How we use your information

  - Provide the core caddie features (distance calculations, club
  recommendations, shot strategy)
  - Send your shot context to the AI coaching service to generate
  personalised advice
  - Sync your data across devices when you sign in
  - Diagnose crashes and improve the app
  - Respond to feedback or support requests

  ## Who we share information with

  Looper uses the following third-party services. Your data is shared only
   as needed to deliver the app's features.

  - **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Crashlytics,
  Analytics, Cloud Functions) — stores your profile, rounds, avatar, and
  feedback. Data processed in Canada (northamerica-northeast1 /
  northamerica-northeast2 regions).
  - **Google BigQuery** — receives aggregated event data (rounds, shots,
  feedback) for analytics. Data warehoused in Canada.
  - **xAI (Grok)** — your shot context and coaching questions are sent to
  Grok to generate AI advice. Coaching messages are stored on Looper's
  servers for up to 90 days, then deleted automatically.
  - **Open-Meteo** — when you start a round, your selected course's
  coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to fetch weather. No personal
  information is shared.
  - **Google Sign-In** — if you sign in with Google, we receive your basic
   Google profile.

  We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with
  advertisers.

  ## How long we keep data

  - **Active account data** (profile, bag, rounds) — kept until you delete
   your account or uninstall the app.
  - **AI coaching messages** — automatically deleted after 90 days.
  - **Crash logs and analytics events** — retained according to Firebase's
   standard retention (up to 14 months for analytics, 90 days for crash
  logs).

  ## Your rights

  - **Access** your data — visible within the app at any time.
  - **Update** your profile, bag, or rounds from within the app at any
  time.
  - **Delete** your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This
   removes your profile, rounds, and avatar from our servers.
  - **Export** your data — contact us at the address below and we'll
  provide your data in JSON.

  If you're a resident of the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or
  California, you may have additional rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA.
   Contact us to exercise them.

  ## Children's privacy

  Looper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
   information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, contact us
  and we'll delete it.

  ## Security

  We use industry-standard security practices including encryption in
  transit (HTTPS) and at rest (Firebase's default encryption). No system
  is perfectly secure; we can't guarantee absolute protection but we
  follow established best practices.

  ## Changes to this policy

  If we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above
  and notify you within the app.
