What Looper does

A personal caddie in your pocket.

Every yardage app shows you a number. Looper shows you the right number, the right club, and the smart way to play the hole.

On-course AI coaching

Looper knows your game. Choose the coaching style that suits you and get setup tips, swing thoughts and real advice tuned to live conditions — or even analyze a photo of a tricky lie.

Course management & strategy

It sees the hole like a caddie and knows how you hit the ball — the smart play, the safe play, or the go-for-it percentage — then tells you which one wins.

Smart club pick

Looper knows every club in your bag and its carry distance, then picks the one that gets you there in today's conditions.

Shot shape & dispersion

Your draw, your fade, your landing zone — drawn right on the map so you can aim where it counts and stay away from trouble.

Plays-like distance

Live wind, elevation, temperature and lie, folded into one honest number — the distance the shot actually plays.

GPS rangefinder + map

Front, middle, back, hazards and landing zones right on the course map. No extra device to carry.

How it works

Set it once. Then play smarter.

1

Build your bag

Tell Looper your clubs and your handicap. It knows your real carry distances, not the marketing ones.

2

Start a round

Pick your course. Looper pulls live weather and maps every hole, tee to green.

3

Trust the number

On every shot you get the conditions-adjusted distance, the smart club, and a caddie to ask when you're not sure.

Off the course

The fastest way to lower strokes.

Looper remembers every round — your handicap trend, where you're losing strokes, and which clubs to trust. The practice you put in finally shows up on the card.

Handicap tracking

Watch your handicap move with every round you improve.

Where you lose strokes

See exactly where a round slips away — off the tee, the approach, or around the green — so you know what to work on.

Round-by-round trends

Every round feeds the next. Looper learns your game and sharpens its advice the more you play.
