What is Looper?

Looper is an AI caddie for your phone. On every shot it gives you the distance that actually matters in the conditions, the smart club for your game, and on-course coaching whenever you want to ask — like a tour caddie who knows how you play.

Which devices does it work on?

Looper is coming to iPhone (iOS) and Android for the 2026 golf season. Want to be among the first to play? Email support@looperai.golf.

How accurate are the distances?

GPS distances are as accurate as your phone's location signal — typically within a few yards in the open. The conditions-adjusted ("plays-like") number is a smart guide for club choice, not a guarantee — always use your own judgement on the course.

Does it work without a signal?

Yes — your distances and the course maps keep working offline once a round is under way, and you'll still have the latest conditions Looper had before you lost signal.

How much does Looper cost?

Looper offers a free tier, plus a paid tier with additional features. Any pricing is shown clearly in the app before you're ever charged.

How is my data handled?

Your rounds and profile are stored securely and sync across your devices. We don't sell your data or share it with advertisers. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This removes your profile, rounds and avatar from our servers. You can also email us and we'll take care of it.

Can I get my data?

Yes. Email support@looperai.golf and we'll send you a copy of your data.
